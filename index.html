01 — About

Multimedia Designer & Visual Storyteller

I'm a multimedia designer based in Catamarca, Argentina, with a multidisciplinary approach that spans branding, social media content, digital design, photography, UI design, and 3D visualization. I work at the intersection of strategy and aesthetics — translating brand ideas into clear, compelling visual systems.

I've worked directly with real clients across the healthcare and retail sectors, managing their digital presence from content strategy through to photography and post design. My personal projects explore brand identity, mobile app UI, and experimental 3D work in Blender and Cinema 4D — always driven by curiosity and a commitment to craft.
